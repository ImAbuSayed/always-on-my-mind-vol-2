[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
[Acoustic guitar strums softly, warm and intimate]
I trace the shadows dancing on your skin
A quiet fire slowly pulling me in
Your midnight whisper is the only sound
I’m lost inside the way we’re spinning round

[Verse 1]
We left the highway miles out of sight
To find this rhythm in the pale moonlight
The coffee’s cold but there’s a burning glow
In every lingering look, in moving slow

[Pre-Chorus]
[Gentle bass drum enters, soft heartbeat rhythm]
I pull you closer, just to feel your breath
A slow surrender with nothing left
The sudden spark when my fingers find yours
A rush of magic I can’t ignore

[Chorus]
[Smooth electric guitar swells, anthemic but intimate]
Cause you are always on my mind
A sweet devotion that I can't unwind
I want to hold you till the morning breaks
And savor every little move you make

[Post-Chorus]
[Breathy, rhythmic acoustic strum]
Always on my mind, yeah
Every little move you make

[Verse 2]
The scent of cedar and a falling rain
Washing away every familiar pain
I watch the firelight reflect in your eyes
A silent promise where our secret lies

[Pre-Chorus 2]
[Bass guitar slides in, deepening the groove]
I pull you closer, just to feel your breath
A slow surrender with nothing left

A rush of magic I can’t ignore

[Chorus]
[Drums pick up, emotionally resonant]
Cause you are always on my mind

[Post-Chorus 2]
[Harmonies build softly]
Always on my mind, yeah
Every little move you make

[Bridge]
[Stripped back, single acoustic guitar picking]
You are the secret that I want to keep
With every heartbeat I am sinking deep

You’ve got Saeed letting go of control
